Set Up Netflix on Apple TV

To connect your Apple TV device to your Netflix account, begin from the home screen and follow the steps below.

  1. From the home screen, select theApp Store.
  2. In the App Store, type "Netflix," then select Netflix to start installation.
  3. Once downloaded, the Netflix app will appear on your home screen.
  4. When the download completes, select Open and follow the instructions on the screen.
    1. Select Sign In to use an existing Netflix account OR sign up to open one.
    2. Enter your email and password to sign in.
      Note:If you're not a member, sign up at Netflix.com.

Your device is now connected to your Netflix account.

Set Up Netflix on LG TV

To connect your LG TV device to your Netflix account, begin from the home screen and follow the steps below.

  1. From the home screen, select theNetflix app.
  2. Select Sign In.
    • If you do not see Sign In, select Yes on the Are you a member of Netflix? screen.
    • If you are not a member, set up your membership
  3. Enter your Netflix email address and password to sign in

Your device is now connected to your Netflix account.

Set Up Netflix on iOS

To access the most up-to-date version of the Netflix app, you'll need an iPhone or iPad using iOS/iPadOS version 18 or later.

Note: If you've downloaded the Netflix app before, you'll be able to redownload it on devices using iOS 12 or later. Learn how to redownload apps from the App Store.

Follow the steps below to get Netflix on your Iphone or Ipad:

  1. Open the App Store.
  2. Select Search.
  3. Type "Netflix" in the search bar and select Netflix from the results.
  4. Select Get to download the app or tap the Cloud button.

Your device is now connected to your Netflix account.

Downloads & Offline Streaming

Netflix allows you to download select movies and TV shows to your device for offline viewing. To download content, you'll need a compatible device and the Netflix app installed. Not all titles are available for download, and the availability of downloads may vary by region.

How to Download

  1. Open the Netflix app.
  2. Select a movie or TV show.
  3. Tap the Download icon.
  4. Wait for the download to finish.
  5. Open the Downloads tab to watch offline.

Important Notes

  • Not every title is available for download.
  • Some downloads expire after a certain period.
  • An internet connection is required to start the download.
  • Downloaded content is stored on the device.

Tip

Connect to Wi-Fi before downloading large files to limit mobile data usage

Subtitle & Audio Settings

Subtitles, audio language and captions can be changed for many shows and movies. For downloads, Netflix shows the two most relevant languages

NOTE: You may see subtitles even when your subtitle settings are off while watching shows.

To use subtitles, captions or choose audio language:

  1. Open Netflix and start playing a show or movie.
  2. Take the action listed in the table below for your device
    Device Action
    Mobile phones or tablets, computers Tap or click the screen
    Smart TV, Blu-ray players,cable boxes, gaming systems Use the remote control to access the settings menu.
    Apple TV Swipe up or press the up arrow on your Apple TV remote.
    Note: The location of the settings menu may vary by device.
  3. At either the top or bottom of the screen, select Audio & Subtitles. On TVs, language options may appear at the bottom without the icon. You can choose from the languages shown or select Other to see all language options.
  4. Make changes to your audio or subtitle selections.

Account & Profile Management

Manage your Netflix account and profile settings.

Account Settings

To access your account settings, follow these steps:

  1. Open Netflix and sign in.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Account" from the dropdown menu.

Profile Settings

To manage your profile settings, follow these steps:

  1. Open Netflix and sign in.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Manage Profiles" from the dropdown menu.

People who live together in a single household can have their own personalized Netflix experience. You can have up to 5 profiles on a single Netflix account.

Note: Each profile has its own watch history, ratings, and recommendations.

Create a Profile

To create a new profile, follow these steps:

  1. Open Netflix and sign in.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Manage Profiles" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click on "Add Profile".
  5. Enter a name for the new profile and select an icon.
  6. Click "Continue" or "Save" to create the profile.

Edit or Delete a Profile

To edit or delete an existing profile, follow these steps:

  1. Open Netflix and sign in.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Manage Profiles" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Click on the profile you want to edit or delete.
  5. Make the desired changes or click "Delete Profile" to remove it.

Parental Controls

Netflix offers parental controls to help manage what content is accessible to children.

Set Up Parental Controls

To set up parental controls, follow these steps:

  1. Open Netflix and sign in.
  2. Click on your profile icon in the top right corner.
  3. Select "Account" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Scroll down to the "Profile & Parental Controls" section.
  5. Select the profile you want to manage.
  6. Click on "Change" next to "Viewing Restrictions".
  7. Enter your Netflix account password if prompted.
  8. Set the desired maturity level and click "Save".

Manage Viewing Restrictions

You can adjust viewing restrictions for each profile at any time by following the same steps as above.
